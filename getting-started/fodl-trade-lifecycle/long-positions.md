# Long positions

Long positions can be defined as Fodls where the supplied asset is a non-stablecoin and the borrowed asset is a stablecoin.&#x20;

The positions are for users who are bullish on market conditions and seek to profit from price appreciation of assets against fiat stablecoins.&#x20;

### Examples

* Supply ETH, borrow DAI.&#x20;
* Supply COMP, borrow USDC.&#x20;
* Supply ETH, borrow USDC
